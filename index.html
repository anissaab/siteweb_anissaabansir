Projects

CataClean

Catamaran that would scan the bottom of lakes and shallow seas. Designed with electronic parts such as Esp32cam, Arduino R3, Funduino, motor DC, and battery. With the data collected, we used YoloV3 to detect if it was garbage, to do that we gathered images of garbage and then create scripts to rotate them to complete the data collection. To move the catamaran, it uses Funduino with I2C communication with two Arduino.

Car with Vision

With my team, we created a self-driving car using image detection with OpenCV and electronic assembly with PicCam v2, motor, Arduino Uno, Raspberry Pi 3B+, and more. The car was able to drive itself between red and yellow plots, using the camera to detect the plots on the ground and make decisions about where to drive. This required a lot of experimentation and tweaking of the camera settings, as well as programming the car to respond to different situations. Overall, it was a challenging but rewarding project that helped me develop my skills in computer vision and hardware design.
